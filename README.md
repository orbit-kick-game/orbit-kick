<h1>Orbit Kick - Launch the Ball, Break the Records</h1>

  <p>Orbit Kick is a compact and addictive soccer arcade game where every kick aims to send the ball farther than before. With simple timing mechanics and meaningful upgrades, each attempt feels rewarding and pushes you to improve your next launch.</p>

  <p>Tap or click at the right moment, upgrade your stats, and watch landmarks fade into the distance as your best records continue to grow.</p>

  <h2>How Orbit Kick Works</h2>
  <p>Each attempt revolves around a power meter that fills automatically. Clicking or tapping at the right moment determines both the strength and angle of the kick. A well-timed shot sends the ball flying and bouncing across the terrain, increasing its total distance.</p>

  <h2>Core Gameplay Loop</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Kick the ball with precise timing</li>
    <li>Earn coins based on distance traveled</li>
    <li>Invest coins into permanent upgrades</li>
    <li>Repeat to reach greater distances</li>
  </ul>

  <img src="https://orbitkick.io/data/image/orbit-kick-io.png" alt="orbit-kick" width="300">

  <h2>World Progression</h2>
  <p>As your distance improves, new environments inspired by famous world landmarks become available. These locations offer visual variety while maintaining the same easy-to-learn mechanics.</p>

  <h2>Upgrade System</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Power increases the strength of the initial kick</li>
    <li>Bounce improves how far the ball travels after hitting the ground</li>
    <li>Offline Earnings generate coins even when you are not playing</li>
  </ul>

  <p>Choosing the right upgrade order helps you progress faster and makes every run more efficient.</p>

  <h2>Controls</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Tap or Click to kick the ball</li>
    <li>Timing determines power and launch angle</li>
    <li>Optional swipes allow minor trajectory adjustments during flight</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Tips for Better Distance</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Upgrade Bounce early for long-term gains</li>
    <li>Maintain steady timing instead of random taps</li>
    <li>Use offline earnings to keep progression consistent</li>
    <li>Frequent upgrades matter more than rare perfect kicks</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Start Kicking Further</h2>
  <p>Orbit Kick turns one simple action into an endless arcade challenge focused on timing, upgrades, and constant improvement. Push your limits, refine your skills, and see how far a single kick can really go.</p>
